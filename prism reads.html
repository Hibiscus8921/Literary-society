About Our Society

The Cambridge Literary Society at LaSalle High School & College was founded by a group of determined and passionate students who believed that literature, expression, and intellectual discussion could shape confident leaders and thoughtful individuals. What started as a small student initiative quickly evolved into a structured academic society recognized within the school community.

Over time, the society has successfully organized literary competitions, debates, public speaking sessions, reading circles, and writing workshops. These activities have encouraged students to discover their voice, develop creativity, and build confidence in communication and leadership.

Today, the Cambridge Literary Society proudly stands as a platform where students learn discipline, teamwork, and academic excellence while nurturing a deep love for literature and expression.
